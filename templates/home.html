Knowledge Synthesizer

From Discussion to Understanding

An AI system that transforms unstructured classroom conversations into structured knowledge, arguments, and visual reasoning maps.

The Problem

Classroom discussions contain valuable ideas, disagreements, and insights — but once the session ends, this reasoning disappears. Notes capture words, not thought structure.

The Solution

Knowledge Synthesizer uses AI to extract concepts, arguments, counterpoints, and open questions from discussions, converting raw speech into structured reasoning.

The Outcome

Instead of flat summaries, students and educators receive interactive knowledge maps that reveal how ideas connect, conflict, and evolve.
